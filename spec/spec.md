# Study Orbit

Study Orbit is an AI learning dashboard inspired by the uploaded wireframe.

## Product
A compact home screen with:
- circular progress/streak indicator
- current lesson card
- next lesson card
- AI coach guidance and weekly milestones

## Tech
- React + Vite
- Express
- OpenRouter free model

## AI requirements
The AI must be user-facing and central. It should generate a study roadmap, current lesson, next lesson, milestones, and motivation as strict JSON.
